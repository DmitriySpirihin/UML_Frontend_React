- `warm-chime.mp3` - Soothing jingling little bells ambience, public domain: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Soothing_jingling_little_bells_ambience.ogg
- `breath-tone.mp3` - Synthetic bell sound, CC0 1.0: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Synthetic_bell_sound.ogg
- `low-bell.mp3` - Gong or bell vibrant, public domain: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Gong_or_bell_vibrant.ogg
- `singing-bowl.mp3` - SingingBowl1, public domain: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:SingingBowl1.ogg
- `gentle-close.mp3` - SingingBowl2, public domain: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:SingingBowl2.ogg
